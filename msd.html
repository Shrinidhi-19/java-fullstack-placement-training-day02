ABOUT MSD

Mahendra Singh Dhoni, born in Ranchi on July 7, 1981, is one of the most successful captains in cricket history. Widely known as "Captain Cool," he is the only captain to win three different limited-overs ICC tournaments. His remarkable journey and unmatched composure continue to inspire millions. 

Beyond his tactical genius, Dhoni revolutionized the role of a lower-order batsman by mastering the art of the run chase. His ability to calculate targets and take matches down to the final over earned him the reputation of cricket's ultimate finisher. Off the field, his transition from a small-town ticket collector to a global sporting icon represents the ultimate story of grit and determination. Even years after stepping away from international cricket, his humility, lightning-fast reflexes behind the stumps, and sharp cricketing brain keep him at the absolute center of Indian cricket folklore.
